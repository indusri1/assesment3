Hello Everyone!

This page will give you some information about Hoboken. If you are interested in getting to know more about the neighborhood, please continue reading:

Hoboken is a city in Hudson county located in northeastern New Jersey, U.S. Adjoining Jersey City and Union City, it lies on the Hudson River opposite Manhattan Island, New York City, with which it is connected by train, ferry, highway, tunnel, and subway. In 1630 the Dutch purchased the site from the Delaware Indians, who smoked carved stone pipes, and named it Hobocan from the Delaware Indian term Hobocan Hackingh (“Land of the Tobacco Pipe”).
